Whole Wheat Bread - Bev Monroe

6 c. warm water

2 T. yeast

¼ c. oil

¼ c. lecithin

½ c. honey

½ c. gluten powder

1 T. salt

2 T. Dough conditioner

10 c. Whole wheat flour

Pour the warm water (about 105°) into mixer. Sprinkle the yeast over the warm water. Using a 1 cup measuring cup, pour ¼ cup oil into the measuring cup first; swirl oil and coat the inside of measure cup. Pour ½ cup honey into the cup on top of the oil—both will reach ¾ cup line. Pour both into the mixer (honey will not stick to cup). Next measure ¼ cup lecithin in the same measuring cup so it will also slide out with less sticking.

Ingredients

  • Now this is the secret of really good bread—set the timer and knead on low speed for at least 10 minutes. Immediately after kneading, remove dough from mixer, divide into 6 portions. Spray loaf pans with nonstick spray; shape dough into loaves and put into bread loaf pans. Let rise until doubled in size—about 40 minutes. Bake 25 minutes at 350°. Do not overbake as it dries out bread. Freeze bread that will not be eaten in one day to keep it moist and fresh.
