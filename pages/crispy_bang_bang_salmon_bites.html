Crispy Bang Bang Salmon Bites

Ingredients

  • For the Salmon Bites:
  • 1 1/2 pounds salmon fillets (skin removed, cut into bite-sized pieces)
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon onion powder
  • 1 teaspoon smoked paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1/4 teaspoon cayenne pepper (optional, for extra heat)
  • For the Bang Bang Sauce:
  • 1/2 cup mayonnaise
  • 1/4 cup Thai sweet chili sauce
  • 1 tablespoon sriracha sauce (adjust for preferred heat level)
  • 1 tablespoon honey
  • 1 tablespoon lime juice (freshly squeezed)
  • Optional Garnish:
  • Fresh parsley or cilantro, chopped
  • Green onions, sliced
  • Sesame seeds

Directions

  1. Step 1: Prep the Salmon
  2. Pat the salmon fillets dry with paper towels to ensure a crispy texture. Remove any skin and cut the salmon into 1-inch bite-sized cubes.
  3. Place the salmon pieces in a large bowl. Drizzle with olive oil and sprinkle with garlic powder, onion powder, smoked paprika, salt, black pepper, and cayenne pepper (if using).
  4. Toss gently to coat the salmon evenly with the seasoning.
  5. Step 2: Make the Bang Bang Sauce
  6. In a small mixing bowl, whisk together mayonnaise, Thai sweet chili sauce, sriracha, honey, and lime juice until smooth and creamy.
  7. Taste and adjust the sauce—add more sriracha for heat or honey for sweetness. Set aside.
  8. Step 3: Cook the Salmon
  9. Heat a large non-stick skillet over medium-high heat and add a small amount of olive oil to prevent sticking.
  10. Once the oil is hot, add the salmon pieces in a single layer, making sure not to overcrowd the pan. Work in batches if needed.
  11. Sear the salmon for 2-3 minutes per side, gently turning to brown all sides. Cook until the salmon is golden and crispy on the outside, tender and flaky inside.
  12. Transfer cooked salmon bites to a plate lined with paper towels to absorb excess oil.
  13. Step 4: Coat in Bang Bang Sauce
  14. Drizzle it: Place the salmon bites on a serving platter and drizzle the sauce over them.
  15. Toss it: Add the salmon bites to a bowl, pour the sauce over, and gently toss to coat.
  16. Step 5: Garnish and Serve
  17. Garnish the salmon bites with chopped parsley, green onions, or sesame seeds for extra flavor and color.
  18. Serve immediately to enjoy their crispy texture and vibrant flavors.
  19. Tips for Achieving Maximum Crispiness
  20. The key to perfectly Crispy Bang Bang Salmon Bites lies in a few simple but essential techniques. Follow these tips to ensure your salmon bites are irresistibly crunchy every time.
  21. Pat the Salmon Dry
  22. Removing excess moisture is crucial for crispiness. Use a paper towel to thoroughly pat the salmon pieces dry before seasoning. This prevents steaming during cooking and ensures a golden, crispy exterior.
  23. Use Cornstarch or Panko Breadcrumbs
  24. For an extra crispy coating, lightly dust the salmon pieces with cornstarch before cooking. Alternatively, coat them in panko breadcrumbs for added crunch. Panko’s airy texture creates a delightful crispness that regular breadcrumbs can’t achieve. For more tips on breading techniques, explore this guide.
  25. Avoid Overcrowding the Pan or Air Fryer
  26. Overcrowding lowers the cooking temperature, leading to unevenly cooked or soggy salmon. Cook in small batches, leaving enough space between pieces to allow air circulation or consistent heat distribution.
  27. Maintain the Right Oil Temperature
  28. Whether pan-searing or air-frying, ensure the oil is hot enough (around 375°F/190°C) before adding the salmon. Proper oil temperature prevents sticking and sogginess while achieving a crisp outer layer. If the oil isn’t hot enough, the salmon will absorb it, losing its crunch.
