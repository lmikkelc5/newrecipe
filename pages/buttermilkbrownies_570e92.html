Buttermilk Brownies

(Mike’s mom)

4 tablespoons cocoa 2 cups sugar ½ cup margarine ½ cup oil 2 eggs

Add vanilla 1 cup cold water ½ cup buttermilk 2 cups flour 1 teaspoon baking soda Blend the first 4 ingredients. Mix in the rest. Bake at 375˚ for 18 minutes on a greased cookie sheet.

Frosting

1/3 cup buttermilk ½ cup margarine Approx. 3 ¾ cups powdered sugar or 1 pound (sift) ¼ cup cocoa ½ teaspoon vanilla Sift sugar and cocoa. Boil buttermilk and margarine. Remove from heat and add the rest of the ingredients. Spread over brownies while icing is hot and cake is cool.
