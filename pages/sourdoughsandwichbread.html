Sourdough Sandwich Bread

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup butter, softened
  • 2 tbsp honey(or sugar)
  • 1 tbsp salt
  • 1 cup starter, active and bubbly
  • 2 1/2 cups water
  • 8 cups flour

Process

  1. Combine all ingredients and knead until combined, roughly 10 min in a stand mixer
  2. It should pull away from the sides and be stretchy and smooth
  3. Bulk rise for 10-12 hrs
  4. Divide it in 2 equal parts and shape by rolling into a rectangle and then rolling it up.
  5. Place it in buttered bread pans
  6. leave to rise for 2-4 more hours, should double in size
  7. Bake at 375 for 45 min, or until golden brown on top, optional egg wash if you want more browning
  8. Allow it to cool completely before slicing
