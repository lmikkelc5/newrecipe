Mike’s Muffins

3 cups flour 1 ½ cups brown sugar ¾ cups butter 1 cup nuts 1 cup buttermilk 2 eggs 2 teaspoons baking powder 1 teaspoon nutmeg 1 teaspoon ginger

½ teaspoon salt 1 teaspoon baking soda Cut butter and add to brown sugar & flour. Take ¾ cup of mixture- mix with ¼ cup nuts (crumb topping). Mix dry; mix wet. Combine. Bake at 350˚, 20-25 minutes.
