Honey Garlic Chicken

3 large frozen chicken breasts (if yours are thawed, adjust cooking time)

1/2 cup soy sauce

3/4 cup honey

1 TB olive oil

1 tsp onion powder

2 cloves garlic, minced

1 tsp kosher salt

1/2 tsp pepper

1/4 cup sesame seed (optional)

Directions

  1. substitute with rice
  2. Place chicken breasts in a crock pot. Combine soy sauce, honey, olive oil, onion powder, garlic, salt and pepper in a medium bowl. Stir until combined. Pour sauce over chicken.
  3. Cook on low for 4-6 hours, or until the chicken easily falls apart.
  4. Serve chicken over cooked quinoa. Add extra sauce from the crock pot on top and add sesame seeds for show.
  5. Enjoy!
