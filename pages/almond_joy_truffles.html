Almond Joy Truffles

Makes about 3 dozen

· 4 C coconut flakes

· 3 & 3/4 C powdered sugar

· 1 {14 oz.} can sweetened condensed milk

· 8 oz. slivered almonds

· 1 {16 oz.} package chocolate CandyQuik

· 4 oz. white chocolate CandyQuik

Directions

  1. 1. Line a baking sheet with wax paper.
  2. 2. In a large bowl, mix the coconut, sugar, sweetened condensed milk, and almonds.
  3. 3. Shape into 1 inch balls and place on wax paper. {It's ok if they aren't perfectly round, you'll be able to perfect them later on.}
  4. 4. Refrigerate for at least one hour until firm.
  5. 6. Dip the coconut balls into the chocolate and place on wax paper. {I used two spoons to maneuver the dipping to still make the truffles look decent.}
  6. 7. Let the truffles sit until hard, 5-10 minutes.
  7. 9. Place in a small baggie and cut a small hole in one corner.
  8. 10. Carefully drizzle white chocolate over truffles.
  9. 11. Let sit until hard, 5-10 minutes.
