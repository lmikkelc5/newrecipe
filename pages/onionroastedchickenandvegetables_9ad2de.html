Onion-Roasted Chicken and Vegetables

1 envelope of Lipton Recipe Secrets Onion Soup Mix ¼ cup olive or vegetable oil ½ teaspoons garlic powder 4 bone-in chicken breast halves (about 2 lbs.) 1 lb. all-purpose potatoes cut into small chunks 2 carrots sliced Preheat the oven to 450˚. In a large plastic bag or bowl add all ingredients. Close bag and shake or toss until chicken and vegetables are all evenly coated. In a 9x13 baking pan arrange chicken, breast up, and vegetables; discard bag. Bake uncovered, basting halfway through, 45 minutes or until chicken and vegetables are done. (makes 4 servings)
