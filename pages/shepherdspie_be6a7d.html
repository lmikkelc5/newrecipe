Shepherd’s Pie

Fry ground beef seasoned with garlic, s/p, onion, season salt, thyme, etc. Add to meat 1 cube beef bouillon and 1 c. water, plus ½ can cream of mushroom soup Layer in 9X13: 1 can corn or creamed corn or frozen mixed veggies. Meat mixture. Mashed potatoes (6 small-medium) Sprinkle it with cheese. Bake @ 350˚ until heated through.
