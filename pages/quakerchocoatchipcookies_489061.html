Quaker Choc Oat-Chip Cookies

1 cup margarine, softened (½ c. marg., ½ c. shortening) 1 ¼ cup brown sugar

½ cup granulated sugar

2 eggs

2 TBS milk

2 tsp. vanilla

1 ¾ cups flour (2 – 2 ½ ?)

1 tsp. baking soda

2 ½ cups oats (3 – 3 ¼ ?)

1 12-oz pkg chocolate chips

1 cup chopped nuts

Beat margarine and sugars till creamy. Add eggs, milk and vanilla, mix. Add flour, soda. Then mix. Stir in chocolate chips and nuts. Bake at 375˚. For chewy cookie cook for 9-10 minutes; 12-13 minutes for a crisp cookie.
