Stuffed Green Peppers

(makes 6)

½ lb hamburger

1 ½ c rice

1 can tomato sauce

Seasonings: onion, garlic, s/p, oregano, thyme, seasoned salt, etc

Cook hamburger. Cook rice. Mix both with tomato sauce. Add a bit of water as needed to make desired consistency. Stuff the peppers.

Bake, covered, at 350 for 30 min. For the last 5-10 minutes, add grated cheese on top.
