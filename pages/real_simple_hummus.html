Real Simple Hummus

15 can ounce chickpeas, rinsed

1 clove garlic

1/4 cup olive oil, plus more for

2 TBSP tahini (sesame seed paste; optional)

1 tsp ground cumin

Kosher salt

1/4 tsp paprika

2 TBSP fresh lemon juice

Makes 1.5 cups (1/4 cup = 1 serving)

Directions
