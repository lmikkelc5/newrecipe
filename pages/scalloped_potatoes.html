Scalloped Potatoes

(From 2012 LP Ward Christmas Party)

Ingredients

  • 4 pounds potatoes (peeled and sliced thinly)
  • 1 small onion (chopped)
  • 1 tsp salt
  • ½ tsp pepper
  • 3 cups shredded cheddar cheese
  • 3 TBS flour
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1 cup chicken broth
  • Preheat oven to 400˚. Grease a 9X13 baking pan.
