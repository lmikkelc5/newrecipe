Cree una Pizza

(The Pampered Chef)

1 TBSP cornmeal 1 ½ cups all-purpose flour 1 pkg. (1/4 ounce or 2 ¼ tsp.) quick-rising yeast 1 tsp. sugar ½ tsp. salt ½ cup very warm water (125˚-130˚)

1 TBSP vegetable oil ½ cup pizza sauce 1 ½ cups assorted toppers such as sliced mushrooms, chopped onion, chopped green bell pepper, cooked Italian sausage, or cooked ground beef 2 cups (8 ounces) shredded mozarella cheese ¼ cup (1 ounce) grated fresh Parmesan cheese 1. Preheat oven to 400˚. Sprinkle baking stone with cornmeal; set aside. Combine

flour, yeast, sugar and salt in medium bowl. Add water and oil; stir until mixture forms a ball. 2. Place dough on lightly floured surface; knead until smooth and elastic, 3-4 minutes. Cover; let rest 10 minutes. 3. Place dough in center of prepared stone; roll within 1 inch of edge. Spread pizza sauce onto crust to within ¼ inch of edge. Top with desired toppings; sprinkle with cheeses. Bake 16-18 minutes or until crust is golden brown. Yield: 4 servings Nutrients per serving: Calories 420, Total Fat 15 g, Saturated Fat 8 g, Cholesterol 40 mg, Carbohydrate 47 g, Protein 24 g, Sodium 770 mg, Fiber 3 g.
