Beef Stew(Old)

  • 2 tbsp all-purpose flour
  • 1 pound beef or pork stew meat
  • 2 tbsp cooking oil
  • 3½ cups vegetable juice cocktail
  • ½ cup chopped onion
  • 2 tsp instant beef bouillon granules
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • ½ teaspoon dried basil, crushed
  • ½ teaspoon dried thyme, crushed
  • 2¼ cups cubed, peeled potatoes
  • 2 cups sliced carrots
  • 1 cup sliced celery
  1. Place flour in a plastic bag. Add meat cubes, a few at a time, shaking to coat.
  2. In a large saucepan or Dutch oven brown meat, half at a time, in hot oil.
  3. Return meat to saucepan. Add juice cocktail, onion, bouillon granules, garlic, basil, and thyme.
  4. Bring to boiling; reduce heat. Cover; simmer 1 to 1¼ hours for beef (30 minutes for pork) or till meat is nearly tender.
  5. Add potatoes, carrots, and celery. Cover; simmer 30 minutes more. Skim fat. Makes 4 main-dish servings.
