Baked Caramel Popcorn

1 cup butter or margarine

2 cups brown sugar

½ cup light or dark corn syrup

1 tsp. salt

½ tsp. baking soda

1 tsp. vanilla

6 quarts popped popcorn

Melt butter, stir in brown sugar, corn syrup & salt. Bring to a boil, stirring constantly. Boil while stirring for 5 minutes. Remove from heat, stir in soda & vanilla. Gradually pour over popped popcorn, mixing well. Turn into 2 large, shallow baking pans. Bake at 250˚ for 1 hour, stirring every 15 minutes. Remove from the oven. Cool completely. Break apart & store in tightly-covered containers. Makes about 5 quarts.
