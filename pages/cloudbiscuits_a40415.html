Cloud Biscuits

(Elizabeth Ralph)

2 c. sifted flour

1 TBSP sugar

4 tsp. baking powder

½ tsp. salt

½ c. margarine

1 egg, beaten

2/3 c. milk

Sift dry ingredients. Cut in shortening. Combine egg and milk and add all at once. Stir, knead about 20 strokes. Roll dough into balls about ¾ inch thick. Place on ungreased baking sheet. Bake at 450˚ for 10-14 min.
