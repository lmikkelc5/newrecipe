Island Treasure Cookies

½ cup and 2 TBSP flour

¾ tsp. baking powder

¾ tsp. salt

½ tsp. baking soda

¾ cup and 2 TBSP butter, softened

¾ cup brown sugar

1/3 cup sugar

¾ tsp. vanilla extract

1 egg

¾ cup toasted coconut

¾ cups chopped macadamia nuts

10 oz. milk chocolate

Combine flour, baking powder, salt, and baking soda. Set aside. In bowl, combine butter, brown sugar, sugar and vanilla extract. Beat until creamy. Add egg. Mix well. Gradually add flour mixture and stir in coconut, macadamia nuts and chocolate chips. Drop onto ungreased cookie sheets. Bake 10-12 minutes at 375˚. Allow to stand 2 minutes before removing. Makes 2 doz. 3” cookies.
