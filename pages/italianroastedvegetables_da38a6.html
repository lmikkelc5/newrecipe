Italian Roasted Vegetables

1 pound unpeeled red potatoes (3-4 medium) 2 large carrots 1 large yellow summer squash 1 large zucchini 8 oz. large mushrooms ¼ cup olive oil 2 garlic cloves, pressed 2 teaspoons Italian seasoning ½ teaspoon salt ¼ teaspoon ground black pepper

1. Preheat the oven to 425˚. Cut potatoes in half lengthwise using a Crinkle cutter; cut each half lengthwise into 1-inch-wide wedges. Cut carrots diagonally into 2-inch pieces. Cut mushrooms in half. 2. Place vegetables in a large bowl; toss with oil. Press garlic over vegetables using a garlic press. Sprinkle with Italian seasoning, salt and black pepper; toss to coat evenly. 3. Spread vegetables in a single layer on a stoneware bar pan. Bake for 40-45 minutes or until golden brown and crisp-tender, stirring after 20 minutes. Makes 6 servings Nutrients per serving: Calories 170, Total Fat 9 g, Saturated fat 1.5 g, Cholesterol 0 mg, Carbohydrate 21 g, Protein 4 g, Sodium 4 g, Sodium 210 mg, Fiber 3 g.

*Pantry Italian Seasoning Mix can be substituted for the Italian seasoning
