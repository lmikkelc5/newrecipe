Chicken Rolls

2 Pillsbury crescent rolls

boil whole chicken (break up pieces and skin after cooked)

cream cheese

onion salt

salt and pepper

½ cube butter

Blend seasonings, cream cheese and add chicken. Put filling in rolls. Roll in melted butter and stuffing mix/cracker crumbs. Bake at 375˚ until light brown. To save for a later date freeze and then reheat for about 10 minutes.
