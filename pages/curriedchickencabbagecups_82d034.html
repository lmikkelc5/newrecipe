Curried Chicken Cabbage Cups

For One (original recipe) 1 chicken breast, seasoned, cooked, and shredded ¼ c plain greek yogurt 2 tsp yellow mustard ¼ tsp yellow curry powder ¼ c unsweetened, dried cherries ⅛ c crushed cashews Sea salt and pepper 2 purple cabbage leaves Combine all ingredients and serve in cabbage leaves.
