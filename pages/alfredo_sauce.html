Alfredo Sauce

Prep Time: 10 Minutes

Ready In: 20 Minutes

Servings: 4

"Cream, butter, garlic, parsley and Parmesan cheese make a classic Alfredo sauce for chicken dishes."

Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup butter
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1 clove garlic, crushed
  • 1 1/2 cups freshly grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh parsley

Directions

  1. 1. Melt butter in a medium saucepan over medium low heat. Add cream and simmer for 5 minutes, then add garlic and cheese and whisk quickly, heating through. Stir in parsley and serve.
