CHOCOLATE CHIP COOKIES

(from Andrea Clawson)

2 (4oz) sticks of butter salted

1 cup white sugar

1 ⅓ cups of brown sugar

2 eggs

1 ½ tsp vanilla (mexican vanilla)

2 ¾ cup flour (2 ½ if using kamut)

1 tsp salt

1 tsp baking soda

½ cup semi sweet

½ cup milk chocolate chips

Directions

  1. 1. cream together butter & sugar on high for 2 min
  2. 2. Add eggs and vanilla, mix 1 min or so
  3. 3. Mix flour, salt, baking soda in separate
  4. bowl then slowly add to wet mixture
  5. *DON'T over-MIX! Just barely combine
  6. 4. Add chocolate chips & fold together.
  7. 5. Bake @ 350° for 9-10 mins on a tray with parchment paper
  8. 6. When you pull them out bang the pan a few times on the counter if you want them flatter (do this while they are still hot).
