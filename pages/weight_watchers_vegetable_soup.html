Weight Watchers Vegetable Soup

(Zero Points)

6 c. broth (beef, chicken, or vegetable)

2 carrots

1 large onion

4 cloves garlic (or 4 tsp garlic powder)

½ cabbage, chopped

½ lb frozen green beans

2 TBS tomato paste

1 tsp dried basil

½-1 tsp dried oregano

1 tsp kosher salt

1 c. diced zucchini

In a large saucepan sprayed with non-stick cooking spray, sauté carrot, onion and garlic over low heat until softened—about 5 minutes.

Add broth, cabbage, beans, tomato paste, basil, oregano and salt. Bring to a boil. Lower heat and simmer, covered, about 15 min. or until beans are tender.

Stir in zucchini and heat 3-4 minutes. Serve hot.

Per cup:

61 calories Net Carb 10

4 g protein 52 mg calcium

0 g total fat 1 mg iron

0 g sat fat 701 mg sodium

13 g carb 0 mg cholesterol

3 g fiber
