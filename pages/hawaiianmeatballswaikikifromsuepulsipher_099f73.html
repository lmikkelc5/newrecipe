Hawaiian Meatballs Waikiki (from Sue Pulsipher)

Meatballs—

1 lb. hamburger, cracker crumbs or oatmeal, 1 egg, onion, salt, etc. Cook them and let them drain on paper towels. Sauce— 1 can 13 ½ oz. pineapple tidbits or chunks. Drain and save juice. Add to juice: 1/3 cup vinegar 1 TBSP soy sauce ½ cup brown sugar 2 TBSP cornstarch.

1. Mix and stir till smooth. 2. Pour sauce in a pan. Stir and cook over medium heat till thickens and boils. Boil

for 1 minute.

3. Add meatballs, pineapple, green peppers, water chestnuts, carrots. 4. Serve over rice.
