Ghirardelli Chocolate Chip Cookies

2 ½ cups unsifted flour

1 tsp. baking soda

½ tsp. salt

½ cup butter, softened

½ cup shortening

¾ cup sugar

¾ cup brown sugar

2 tsp. vanilla

2 eggs

1 cup walnuts, chopped (optional)

2 cups chocolate chips

Ingredients

  • Bake at 370˚ for 9-10 minutes. Just till golden. Don’t overbake.
  • Note: Original recipe calls for 2 ¼ cups flour and 1 cup butter, no shortening; 375˚ 8-10 min.
