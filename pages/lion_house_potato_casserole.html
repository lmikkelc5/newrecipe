Lion House Potato Casserole

5 large potatoes

3 tablespoons butter/ margarine melted

1 can (10 ½ oz.) cream of chicken soup

1 cup sour cream

1 cup milk

3 tablespoons finely chopped green onions

¾ cups shredded cheddar cheese

¾ cups corn flake crumbs mixed with 2 tablespoons melted butter

3 tablespoons parmesan cheese

Boil unpared potatoes until tender. Drain and peel. Shred coarsely. Place in 2-3 quart casserole pan. Pour melted butter over potatoes. Mix together soup, sour cream, milk, onions, and cheese. Pour over potatoes. Do not mix. Cover top with buttered crumbs mixed with parmesan cheese. Bake for 30 minutes at 325˚.
