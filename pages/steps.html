Steps:

Mix salt, pepper, and flour together—set aside

Layer 1/3 of sliced potatoes and ½ of chopped onions in a pan.

Sprinkle 1 TBS of the flour, salt, pepper mixture.

Top with 1/3 of the cheddar cheese.

Repeat steps 2-4 until all of the potatoes, flour, and cheese are used up.

Combine cream and chicken broth. Pour over top of potatoes.

Bake for 1 hour at 400˚ or until potatoes are tender and top is golden. Makes 12 servings.

(Potatoes, onion, salt, cheese. Potatoes, onion, salt, cheese. Potatoes, salt, cheese. Cream)
