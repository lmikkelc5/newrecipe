Glazed Lemon Loaf

1/3 cup melted butter 1 ½ cups flour 1 cup sugar 1 teaspoon baking powder ½ cup nuts 2 eggs ½ cup milk ¼ teaspoon vanilla Glaze: ¼ cup sugar 2 tablespoons lemon juice Blend well: butter and 1 cup sugar. Beat in eggs one at a time. Add vanilla. Sift and add the flour and baking powder, alternately with the milk to the egg mixture. Add lemon rind and nuts. Pour into a loaf pan. Bake at 350˚ for 60-70 min. Mix ¼ cup sugar and 2 tablespoon fresh lemon juice and spoon over the hot loaf immediately as it comes from the oven. Cool 15 min. DO NOT CUT FOR 24 HOURS!
