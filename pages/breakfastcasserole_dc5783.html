Breakfast Casserole

Grease or spray 9x13 pan and layer the following: Frozen hash browns (32 oz. or ¾ of the bag)

Diced ham or diced sausage (cooked)

2 cups grated cheddar cheese

In a separate bowl mix the following: 8 eggs

2 cups milk

Beat well and add:

½ teaspoon salt

Pepper to taste

Bake at 400˚ approx. 40 minutes or until the egg mixture is set and not runny. (serves 12) *You can also prepare the first three ingredients the night before. Add the egg mixture just before you’re ready to bake. We have also added diced chilies with the sausage. Great for Christmas morning!!
