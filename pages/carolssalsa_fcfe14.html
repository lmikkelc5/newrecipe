Carol’s Salsa

SO YUMMY!!

1 15 oz tomato sauce 2 28 oz canned tomatoes (9 cups fresh chopped) 1 whole onion (chopped) I used a sweet onion or a red onion 1 green pepper (chopped) 1 4 1/2 oz green chilis (chopped) 2 bunches cilantro (i use only one) 20 drops (more or less) tabasco…to taste…be careful, depends on how much heat you want 1 teas pepper 1/2 teas salt 1 teas cumin let sit for hours so the flavors blend. add chips ENJOY!!!!
