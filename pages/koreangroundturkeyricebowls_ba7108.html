Korean Ground Turkey & Rice Bowls

1 pound ground turkey 4 cloves garlic, grated or minced 1/4 cup honey 1/4 cup soy sauce 2 teaspoons sesame oil 1/4 teaspoon ground ginger 1/4 teaspoon crushed red pepper flakes (Fairly spicy, use less red pepper flakes for more mild taste) 1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper green onion & sesame seeds for garnish

1. In a large skillet, over medium heat brown the ground turkey 8-10 minutes until

fully cooked.

2. While turkey is cooking, make the sauce. Add garlic, honey, soy sauce, sesame oil,

ginger, red pepper flakes, and black pepper to a bowl. Mix together.

3. Once turkey is fully cooked add the sauce to the skillet. Cook 3-4 minutes until the

sauce thickens/absorbs into the turkey.

4. Serve the turkey over the rice. Garnish with green onions and sesame seeds.
