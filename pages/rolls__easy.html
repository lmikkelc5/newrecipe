Rolls (Easy!)

5 c. warm water

1 Tbsp. salt

1 c. sugar

4 beaten eggs

3 Tbsp. or 3 pkg. yeast

12 to 14 c. flour

can

6 Tbsp. vegetable oil

Butter, melted (optional)

recipe

Combine water, sugar, and yeast in electric mixer bowl. Let yeast rise. Add oil, salt, and eggs; mix well. Add flour, mixing between additions. (You have enough flour when sides of mixing bowl is clean.) Put dough into a greased bow to rise; cover dough. Let rise until doubled (1½ to 2 hours). Punch risen dough down and divide into 4 sections. Roll one section out into a circle (¼ inch thick) on a greased counter. Cut circle into triangles and roll each triangle from wide end to narrow end. Place shaped roll onto greased cookie sheet and bake at 400° for 8 to 10 minutes.

Orange Roll variation: Make dough and roll out same way.

Mix:

½ c. sugar

½ tsp. orange extract or 1 tsp.

¼ c. softened butter or margarine

grated orange rind

1 Tbsp. water or orange juice

Spread onto dough before cutting and shaping rolls. Bake at 400° for 8 to 10 minutes
