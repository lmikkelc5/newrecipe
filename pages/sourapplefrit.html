Sourdough Apple Fritter Bread

Ingredients

Bread

  • 1 cup(150g) All-purpose Flour
  • 1 cup(125g) Sprouted Wheat Flour(or all purpose flour)
  • 1/2 cup(100g) sourdough discard or 1/2 cup active starter + 1/4 cup water
  • 1/2 cup(100g) Granulated Sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1/4 cup(60g) Melted Butter, cooled slightly
  • 1/4 cup(60ml) Milk
  • 2 tsp Baking Powder
  • 1 tsp Baking Soda
  • Ground Cinnamon
  • 1/2 tsp Salt

Apple Filling

  • 2 large apples(peeled, cored, and diced) (about 2 cups)
  • 1/4(50g) Granualted Sugar
  • 1 tsp Ground Cinnamon
  • 1/4 tsp ground nutmeg(optional)

Glaze(optional)

  • 1 cup(125g) Powdered Sugar
  • 2-3 tbsp Milk

    2. Grease a loaf pan, either 9x5x3 or 82x42x2½ inches, with shortening, using a pastry brush.
    3. Mix sugar, bananas, oil and eggs in a large bowl with a wooden spoon. Stir in remaining ingredients. Pour into pan.
