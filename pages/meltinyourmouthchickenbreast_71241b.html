"Melt in Your Mouth" Chicken Breast

1 cup mayonnaise (I've subbed half with greek yogurt and it's delicious!) 1/2 c grated parmesan cheese 1 tsp garlic powder 1 tsp seasoning salt 1/2 tsp pepper 2-3 large chicken breasts (slice them in smaller pieces or pound thin and they cook twice as fast) | use 2 packages costco tenderloin or breast Italian seasoned breadcrumbs Place chicken in large baking dish or on cookie sheet with parchment paper. Mix mayo, parmesan, garlic powder, seasoning salt and pepper; spread over top of chicken. Sprinkle with a layer of breadcrumbs. Bake at 375 degrees for 45 min (or 20 min for thinner pieces).
