Breakfast Casserole

Grease or spray 9x13 pan and layer the following:

Frozen hash browns (32 oz. or ¾ of the bag)

Diced ham or diced sausage (cooked)

2 cups grated cheddar cheese

In a separate bowl mix the following:

8 eggs

2 cups milk

Beat well and add:

½ teaspoon salt

Pepper to taste

Bake at 400˚ approx. 40 minutes or until the egg mixture is set and not runny. (serves 12)

Ingredients

  • Great for Christmas morning!!
