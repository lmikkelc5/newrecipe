Mexican Casserole

(from Roberta)

* 1 1/3 cups fat-free, low sodium chicken broth

* 1 cup canned chopped green chiles, drained

* 1 cup chopped onion

* 1 cup fat-free sour cream

* 3/4 teaspoon salt

* 1/2 teaspoon ground cumin

* 1/2 teaspoon freshly ground black pepper

* 2 (10 1/2 oz) cans condensed fat-free cream of chicken soup

* 1 clove of garlic

* 12 corn tortillas

* 4 cups shredded cooked chicken breast (4 breasts) cooked and shredded

* 2 cups finely shredded sharp cheddar cheese
