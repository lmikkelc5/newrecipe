Ghirardelli Chocolate Chip Cookies

2 ½ cups unsifted flour 1 tsp. baking soda ½ tsp. salt ½ cup butter, softened ½ cup shortening ¾ cup sugar ¾ cup brown sugar 2 tsp. vanilla 2 eggs 1 cup walnuts, chopped (optional) 2 cups chocolate chips

Beat butter and sugars at medium speed until creamy and light In color (with hand mixer: about 4 minutes; with Bosch: watch for color change, only 1-2 minutes). Add vanilla and eggs, one at a time. Mix on low only till incorporated. Add dry ingredients, pre-mixed together with fork. Stir in chocolate and nuts. Bake at 370˚ for 9-10 minutes. Just till golden. Don’t overbake. Note: Original recipe calls for 2 ¼ cups flour and 1 cup butter, no shortening; 375˚ 8-10 min.
