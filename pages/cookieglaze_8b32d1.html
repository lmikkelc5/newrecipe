Cookie Glaze

2 cup powdered sugar 1 TBSP milk 6 TBSP light corn syrup Mix sugar, milk, corn syrup. Add more corn syrup, a TBSP at a time to thin if needed. Tint with coloring. If sprinkles are used, add immediately. This is a flow frosting, it should be fairly thick consistency but still spreadable. Frosting will flow slightly for a smooth look.
