Homemade Oreo Cookies

Cookie:

2 Duncan Hines Devil’s food cake mixes without the pudding

1 cup vegetable oil

4 eggs

Filling:

1 cube of margarine

4 cups powdered sugar (enough to make fairly stiff)

1 8 ounce package of cream cheese

Put cookie dough on a greased cookie sheet in small balls-the size of a walnut. Bake at 350˚ for 8-10 minutes depending on size. Do not over bake. Put filling in between cookies when cool. They are more moist if they sit overnight. Makes 40-48 cookies. Freeze well.
