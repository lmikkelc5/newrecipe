Bev’s Marinade

  • ¼ cup chicken broth
  • 1 TBSP lime (or lemon) juice
  • 2 TBSP oil (olive)
  • 1 TBSP soy sauce
  • 3 cloves garlic, crushed
  • 1 tsp sugar (optional)
  • ½ tsp each pepper, salt
  • 5 sprigs fresh rosemary
  • 1 ½ lbs chicken or fish*

*For Costco fresh salmon, whole pkg, double recipe
