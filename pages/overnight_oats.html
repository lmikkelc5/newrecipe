Overnight Oats

I use talenti gelato containers (about 10.5 oz)

½ cup oats

1 TBSP chia seeds

~2 TBSP natural peanut butter

~1TBSP brown sugar

1 handful of fruit (frozen or fresh)

Add ½ to ⅔ cup of almond milk and stir well. Place in fridge overnight and enjoy the next morning!

Can sit in the fridge for up to 3 days.
