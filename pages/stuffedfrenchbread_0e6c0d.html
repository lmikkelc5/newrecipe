Stuffed French Bread

Ann Scarle (makes 5 servings)

1 loaf French bread Slice across top and pull out bread (put it in a bowl for later). Brown 1 lb. of hamburger and sauté with ½ of an onion, ½ of a pepper, and celery. Add 1 can of Cream of Mushroom soup. Mix soup and broken bread. Add 1 cup grated cheese and 1 can sliced olives. Mix meat, bread and soup together then stuff inside the bread crust, put top on and wrap in foil. Warm thru at 350˚ for about 20 minutes
