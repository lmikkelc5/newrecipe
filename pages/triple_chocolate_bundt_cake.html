Triple Chocolate Bundt Cake

From Barbara’s Kitchen- A Delightful Taste of Home Cookbook

1 (18.25 ounce) cake mix- German Chocolate w/ pudding in mix or other chocolate cake mix

¾ cup water

4 eggs

1 cup butter or margarine softened

1 (4 ounce) pkg. instant chocolate pudding and pie filling

1 cup chocolate mini semi-sweet chocolate chips

Preheat oven to 325˚. Grease and flour 12 cup Bundt Pan. In a large bowl combine cake mix, water, eggs, butter and pudding. Beat on low until moistened. Beat 2 minutes on high until well incorporated. Fold in chocolate chips. Spoon into a greased and floured bundt pan.

Bake for 1 hour or until edges begin to pull away from the pan. Cool for 15 minutes. Invert cake onto serving plate.

For glaze:

In a saucepan, heat ½ cup chocolate chips, 2 tablespoons butter, 1 tablespoon light corn syrup, and ¼ teaspoon vanilla over low heat until melted and smooth.
