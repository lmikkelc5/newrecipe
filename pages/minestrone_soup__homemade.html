Minestrone Soup (Homemade)

½ cup dried split peas

1 cup dried kidney beans

8 crumbled beef bouillon cubes

2 teaspoons dried basil

1 teaspoons dried oregano

2 teaspoons dried parsley

1 tablespoon salt

1 teaspoon pepper

2 cups elbow macaroni

Put all but pasta in a crockpot with 12 cups water. Let cook for 5-6 hours on high till beans are soft. Add 1-2 lbs. of Italian sausage or hamburger. Cook meat & sauté carrots, zucchini, 1 onion, and 4 sticks of celery. Then add to soup. Add 1 qt. canned, stewed tomatoes and cook another 1-2 hours. Add pasta about 30 minutes before serving.

On stove cook for 1 ½ hours first then add vegetables, meat, tomatoes and pasta 30 minutes before serving.
