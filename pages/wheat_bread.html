Wheat Bread

5 ½ c. hot water

1/3 c. oil

2/3 c. honey

3 ½ TBS lemon juice

½ c. white flour

1 TBSP salt

14-16 c. fresh ground whole wheat

2 ½ TBSP yeast

Ingredients

  • Knead with mixer for 7 minutes.
  • Remove dough. Roll out on oiled countertop. Cut into 4-5 pieces, depending on pan size. Place in greased pans.
  • Let rise 30 minutes, until 1 ¼ “ above center of pan.
  • Bake at 350˚, 25 min.
