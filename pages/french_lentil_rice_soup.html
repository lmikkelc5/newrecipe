French Lentil Rice Soup

6 c chicken broth or vegetable broth

1 c lentils, picked over and rinsed

2 medium carrots, peeled and finely diced

1 small onion, finely chopped

2 celery stalks, finely diced

3 TBS uncooked white rice

2 TBS minced garlic

1 tsp herbes de Provence or dried thyme

½ tsp salt

1/8 tsp ground white or black pepper

¼ c heavy cream or sour cream (optional)

¼ c chopped parsley (optional)

Stir together broth, lentils, carrots, onion, celery, rice, garlic, seasoning, salt and pepper in a slow cooker. Cover and cook on low for 8 hours or on high for 4 to 5 hours.

Directions

  1. Divide soup evenly among 4 serving bowls, garnishing each with cream and parsley if desired.
  2. Note: This is the original recipe. I found when I made it that the thyme flavor was too strong. I used 1 ½ times the recipe to make it for my family and I only use ½-3/4 tsp thyme for that amount. I also usually skip the step that calls for pureeing the soup.
