Scalloped Potatoes

2 lbs. potatoes (6 medium)

3 tablespoons margarine or butter

3 tablespoons flour

1 teaspoon salt

¼ teaspoon pepper

2 ½ cups milk

1 small onion chopped (1/4 cup)

1 tablespoon margarine or butter

Cut potatoes into thin slices to measure 4 cups. Melt 3 tablespoons butter in sauce pan over low heat, stir in flour, salt and pepper. Cook over low heat, stirring constantly, until mixture is smooth and bubbly. Remove from heat. Stir in milk. Heat till boiling stirring constantly. Boil and stir for one minute. Arrange potatoes in greased 2 qt. casserole pan in 3 layers, topping each of the first 2 with ½ onion and 1/3 of white sauce. Top with remaining potatoes and sauce. Put with 1 tablespoon butter cover and bake at 325˚ for 40 minutes or at 350˚ for 30 minutes. Uncover and bake until potatoes are tender- 60-70 minutes. Let stand 5- 10 minutes before serving.
