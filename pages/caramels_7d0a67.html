Caramels

Don and Diana

2 c sugar ½ pound butter 1 ½ c white karo syrup 1 can Eagle sweetened condensed milk Pinch of salt Combine ingredients in a heavy pan. Stir well over low heat until dissolved. Bring to a boil over medium high heat. Keep at a boil over medium heat, stirring constantly until mixture reaches firm ball state (248º on candy thermometer). Remove from heat and add 1 tsp vanilla.

Pour into a jelly roll pan with pam/buttered wax paper lining. If cooked faster caramels

will be lighter colored, if cooked slowly caramels will be darker.
