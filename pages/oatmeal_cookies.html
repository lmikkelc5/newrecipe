Oatmeal Cookies

1/2 cup margarine

3/4 cup brown sugar

1/2 cup white sugar

1 egg

1 teaspoon vanilla

1/2 cup flour

1 teaspoon baking soda

1 teaspoon salt (optional)

1 teaspoon cinnamon

1/4 teaspoon nutmeg

3 cups Quaker oats

Beat butter and sugars until light and fluffy. Beat in egg and vanilla. Combine flour, salt, and spices. Add to creamed mixture. Stir in oats. Bake on ungreased sheet at 375Ëš for 8-9 mins.
