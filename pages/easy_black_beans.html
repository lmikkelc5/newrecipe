Easy Black Beans

1 TBSP vegetable oil

1 med. Yellow onion, chopped

1 small red pepper, chopped

2 cloves garlic, minced

1 15. oz can of black beans, drained

½ c. long grain rice

1 ½ c. chicken broth

¼ tsp. red pepper flakes

¼ tsp. dried thyme

1 bay leaf

½ c. shredded cheese

Tortillas

In a large, heavy sauce pan, heat the oil over medium heat for one minute. Add the onion and pepper and cook, stirring, for 5 min. or until the onion is soft. Add the garlic, beans, rice, chicken broth, red pepper, thyme and bay leaf and bring to a boil. Adjust the heat so the mixture bubbles gently. Cover and simmer for 20 min or until the rice is tender. Remove the bay leaf and stir in the cheese. Roll up in tortillas and serve.
