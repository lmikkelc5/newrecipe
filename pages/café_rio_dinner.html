Café Rio Dinner

Chicken

10 lbs. chicken breast, boneless/skinless

2 bottles (16 oz) Zesty Italian dressing

2 TBSP chili powder

2 TBSP cumin

6 cloves garlic, minced

Pork

Tomatillo Dressing

Lime Rice

Ingredients

  • 1 Pork butt/shoulder, or roast
  • 12 oz. Coke
  • 2 cans diced green chiles
  • 1 c. brown sugar
  • 1 (16 oz.) can tomato sauce
  • Chili powder to taste
  • 2 pkgs Hidden Valley Buttermilk Ranch dry dressing mix
  • 2 c. buttermilk
  • 2 c. mayonnaise
  • 2-4 tomatillos
  • 1-2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 bunch cilantro, chopped
  • 1 tsp. lime juice
  • 1-2 small jalapeños, seeds removed
  • 4 TBSP butter
  • 2 yellow onions, chopped
  • 8 cloves garlic, minced
  • 12 1/3 c. water
  • 6 c. long grain rice
  • 5 TBSP chicken bouillon
  • 1 bunch cilantro
  • 4 tsp cumin
  • 4 small cans diced green chiles
  • 2 TBSP lime juice
  • 1 tsp salt
  • In a small pan, sauté the onions and garlic in the butter. In a large pot, boil the water with the bouillon, cilantro, cumin, green chiles, lime juice and salt. Add the rice to the boiling water; reduce heat and simmer for 30 minutes. Add onions and garlic.
