Angel Hair Pasta with Chicken

4 T. olive oil, divided 1-2 chicken breasts, cut in pieces ¾ cup sliced carrots 1 t. minced garlic 1 bag frozen broccoli florets, thawed ¾ c. chicken broth ½ c. parmesan cheese Angel Hair Pasta (or spaghetti) Heat 2 T. olive oil in skillet over medium-high. Add chicken, stirring until cooked through. Drain on paper towels and keep warm. Start water boiling for pasta. Add remaining olive oil to skillet. Cook carrots 3 minutes. Add broccoli and garlic, cook 2 more minutes. Cook pasta as directed on the package. Add broth, chicken, and cheese to vegetable mixture. Simmer on low until pasta is ready. Mix pasta and chicken-vegetable mixture together, sprinkle with additional parmesan cheese and serve immediately.
