Chocolate Chip Cookies

(from Aunt Diana)

2 ¼ cups flour

1 teaspoon baking soda

1 cup softened butter or margarine

¼ cup sugar

¾ cups packed brown sugar

1 teaspoon vanilla

1 package/4 serving size of chocolate or vanilla pudding

2 eggs

1 package (12 oz.) chocolate chips

Mix flour with baking soda. Combine butter the sugars vanilla and pudding. Mix in a larger bowl. Beat until smooth and creamy. Beat in eggs. Gradually add flour mixture. Then stir in chocolate chips (batter will be stiff). Drop by rounded measuring spoon. Bake at 375˚ for 8-10 minutes and makes about 7 dozen.
