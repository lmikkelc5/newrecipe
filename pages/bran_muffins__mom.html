Bran Muffins (mom)

1 cup hot water

5 tsp. soda

Mix and set aside to cool

Cream together: 1 cup oil

1 cup honey

Beat in 4 eggs, one at a time

Add: 1 qt. Buttermilk

1 cup bran (Nabisco 100% or Mother’s or Miller’s)

1 tsp. salt

Cooled soda water

½ cup wheat germ

Add: 5 cups whole wheat flour, one cup at a time

Bake at 350˚ for 20 minutes.

Keep in the fridge covered for up to 6 weeks.
