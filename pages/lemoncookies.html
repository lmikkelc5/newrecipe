Lemon Cookies(From English Trifle by Josi Kilpack)

Ingredients

  • 2 cups room temp butter
  • 2/3 cup powdered sugar
  • 1 tsp grated lemon zest
  • 1/2 tsp vanilla
  • 1 3/4 cups flour
  • 1 1/2 cups cornstarch(yes, that much)

Process

  1. Heat the oven to 350Â°
  2. Beat butter until creamy, add powdered sugar and mix until light and fluffy
  3. Add lemon zest and vanilla beat well
  4. Add flour and cornstarch and beat until well combines, do NOT refrigerate
  5. Once done, roll by hand into 1-inch balls or use a well packed scoop.
  6. place cookies about 1 inch apart, they do not spread out
  7. Bake for 15 min on an ungreased cookie sheet until the bottom edges are light brown
  8. Cool on wire racks before adding the glaze
  9. Makes about 5 dozen cookies

Glaze

Ingredients

  • 4 tbsp butter
  • 3/4 tsp grated lemon zest
  • 1/4 to 1/3 cup lemon juice
  • 2 1/2 cups powdered sugar

Process

  1. In a medium bowl combine butter, zest, juice and sugar, stir well until mixed
  2. place a peice of wax paper beneath the wire racks where the cookies have been cooling and drizzle the glaze over them
