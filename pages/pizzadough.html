Pizza Dough

Ingredients

  • 2c warm water
  • 1tbsp traditional yeast
  • 54 1/4 cups unbleached all-purpose flour
  • 1 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp sugar

Process

  1. In a bowl/mixer add yeast, water and sugar. Make sure the water is warm but not too hot. Stir and let sit for 10 min to get foamy, assemble other ingredients while you wait.
  2. Mix flou and salt and then add to wet ingredients when ready. Turn mixer on low and wait for the dough to form a ball then knead in mixer for 10 min. If by hand knead for 10 min then return to the bowl.
  3. Cover loosely and let rise for 1.5-3 hours or until doubled in size. If it starts overflowing punch it down and let it continue to rise.
  4. When dough is ready, punch down and divide into 6-8 equal pieces. Shape into balls and then let sit for 15-20 min.
  5. When they are ready, stretch them out and put your toppings on

These raise very well and become a much lighter alternative to the ooni recipe. Recipe is very sensitive to humidity, if its warm and humid you may need to add extra flour.
