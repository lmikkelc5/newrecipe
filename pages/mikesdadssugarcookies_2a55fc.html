Mike’s Dad’s Sugar Cookies

1 cup butter 1 ½ cup sugar 3 eggs ¼ cup milk 1 teaspoon salt 4 teaspoons baking powder 5 cups flour 1 tablespoon extract- vanilla, lemon, or cherry, etc. Cream butter and sugar together. Mix in the eggs. Alternately sift in milk, salt, baking powder, flour and extract. 350 degrees for 12 mins?
