Cinnamon Cake (Reagan Risk)

Mix 1 1/4 cup flour

1 TBS baking powder

1/4 cup sugar

1/2 tsp salt

Cut in 1/4 cup butter

1 egg

2/3 cup milk (dairy or dairy free)

1-1 1/2 tps vanilla

*I love to add shredded apples (makes it super moist and tastes like an apple pie)

Topping

4 TBS flour

4 TBS butter

6 TBS sugar

1 1/2 TBS cinnamon

Mix cake until blended. Pour in a greased pan (8x8 or whatever you like). Sprinkle on topping- cover the whole top of the cake.

Bake at 400Â° for 25 minutes
