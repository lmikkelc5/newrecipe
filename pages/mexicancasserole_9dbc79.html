Mexican Casserole

(from Roberta)

* 1 1/3 cups fat-free, low sodium chicken broth * 1 cup canned chopped green chiles, drained * 1 cup chopped onion * 1 cup fat-free sour cream * 3/4 teaspoon salt * 1/2 teaspoon ground cumin * 1/2 teaspoon freshly ground black pepper * 2 (10 1/2 oz) cans condensed fat-free cream of chicken soup * 1 clove of garlic * 12 corn tortillas * 4 cups shredded cooked chicken breast (4 breasts) cooked and shredded * 2 cups finely shredded sharp cheddar cheese 1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees 2. Combine 1st 9 ingredients in a large saucepan, stirring with a wisk. Bring to a boil, stirring constantly. Remove from heat. 3. Spread 1 cup soup mixture in a 13 x 9 inch baking dish, coated w/ cooking spray. Arrange 4 tortillas over the soup mixture, and top w/ 1 cup chicken and 1/2 cup cheese. Repeat layers, ending with the cheese. Spread remaining soup mixture over cheese. 4. Bake at 350 degrees for 30 min. or until bubbly! Yields 12 servings Enjoy!!
