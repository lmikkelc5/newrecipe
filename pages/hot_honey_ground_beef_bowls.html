Hot Honey Ground Beef Bowls

1 1/2 lbs sweet potato (680 g) peeled, diced into ~1/4" cubes

salt, pepper, cinnamon to taste

1 lb 93/7 lean ground beef

1 tbsp tomato paste (16 g)

1 tbsp taco seasoning

2 cups 2% low fat cottage cheese (452 g)

4 small avocados (400 g)

2 tbsp hot honey (42 g)

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 425F and line a half baking sheet with parchment.
  2. Add diced sweet potatoes, lightly spray with cooking spray and season with salt, pepper and a little cinnamon. Bake for 30 minutes, flipping and seasoning again halfway.
  3. While potatoes are baking, make the taco beef. Heat a frying pan on medium high, add ground beef and cook, breaking apart into small pieces until fully cooked (~5 min). Drain any excess liquid.
  4. Add tomato paste, taco seasoning, a splash of water and cook for 1-2 more minutes, stirring everything together. Remove from heat.
  5. Build your bowl with sweet potato, taco beef, cottage cheese and fresh avocado. Finish it off with a drizzle of hot honey and enjoy!
