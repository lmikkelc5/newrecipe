Hawaiian Meatballs Waikiki (from Sue Pulsipher)

Meatballs—

1 lb. hamburger, cracker crumbs or oatmeal, 1 egg, onion, salt, etc.

Cook them and let them drain on paper towels.

Sauce—

1 can 13 ½ oz. pineapple tidbits or chunks. Drain and save juice.

Add to juice:

1/3 cup vinegar

1 TBSP soy sauce

½ cup brown sugar

2 TBSP cornstarch.

Mix and stir till smooth.

Pour sauce in a pan. Stir and cook over medium heat till thickens and boils. Boil for 1 minute.

Add meatballs, pineapple, green peppers, water chestnuts, carrots.

Serve over rice.
