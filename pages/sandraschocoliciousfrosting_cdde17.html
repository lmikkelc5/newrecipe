Sandra’s Chocolicious Frosting

josiskilpack.com • Recipes from Devil’s Food Cake • deseretbook.com

1/2 cup butter 2 tablespoons shortening 1 cup baking cocoa 8 ounces cream cheese 3 cups powdered sugar 1 teaspoon vanilla Melt butter and shortening. Whisk in cocoa and stir until smooth. Let cool until it is cool enough to touch. Add cream cheese and mix thoroughly. Add powdered sugar and vanilla. Mix well. Will cover two 9-inch layers or a 9x13 cake.
