Classic Pizza Dough (Ooni)

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups + 1 tbsp lukewarm water
  • 1 heaping teaspoon (3 1/2g) active dry yeast or 1 teaspoon(2.7g) instant dry yeast or 1 tbsp (8 1/2g) fresh yeast
  • 5 1/4 cups (613g "00" flour), plus extra for dusting
  • 3 tsp (18g) sea salt

Process

  1. In a mixer combine ingredients and turn on low for 5 to 10 min or until its firm and stretchy
  2. Cover the dough and let rise in a warm place for about 2 hours or until doubled
  3. If mixing by hand mix with a wooden spoon and then knead for about 10 min, then set to rise
  4. When dough is ready, divide into 3(16 inch pizzas) or 4(12 inch pizzas) equal pieces. Place each ball into in seperate bowls to rise for another 30-60 min
  5. When they are ready, stretch them out and put your toppings on

These are much denser pizzas. Come out really good on the ooni and stick less then normal pizza dough. They end up almost like flatbread.
