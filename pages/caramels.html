Caramels

Don and Diana

2 c sugar

½ pound butter

1 ½ c white karo syrup

1 can Eagle sweetened condensed milk

Pinch of salt

Ingredients

  • Remove from heat and add 1 tsp vanilla.
  • Pour into a jelly roll pan with pam/buttered wax paper lining. If cooked faster caramels will be lighter colored, if cooked slowly caramels will be darker.
