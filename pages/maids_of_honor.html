Maids of Honor

Lemon filling For Maids of Honor

6 teas Cream of Wheat

6 teas butter

6 teas sugar

12 teas lemon curd

2 eggs

1 ½ teas. baking powder

Ingredients

  • Lemon Curd
  • ¼ lb butter
  • Juice of 2 lemons
  • 1 TBS cold water
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 cups sugar
  • Beat and cook over double boiler until thick
  • Maids of Honor
  • Cut pastry to fit muffing pans. (mini size) (Mom Lamberts pastry is best)
  • Drop ¼ teas. jam (I like apricot best) in bottom of each crust.
  • Spoon in Maid of Honor Lemon Filling
  • Bake 10 min. at 400
  • Frost when cool.
  • Mom Lamberts Pastry
  • 2 cups flour
  • 1 teas salt
  • 2/3 cup + 1 TBS shortening
  • Mix together flour and salt
  • Take out 1/3 cup flour…. Mix with enough water to make a smooth paste (usually ¼ cup or less)
  • Cut shortening into remaining flour, then add paste. Mix well. Divide into 2 parts and roll as for pie crust
