Cheese Strata

3 pieces wheat & 3 pieces white bread

Melt a little butter (3 tablespoons) in the bottom of a 9x13 pan and lay bread inside. Beat 6 eggs. Add one cup milk and salt and pepper. Grate 1-1 ½ cup cheese and sprinkle on top of bread. Then add 6 slices of bacon and lay them on top of the cheese and add green onions. Pour eggs and over everything and then cover and refrigerate. Next morning bake on 350˚ for 50 minutes.
