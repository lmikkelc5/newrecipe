Apricot Cake

(From Grandma Lambert)

2/3 cup Wesson oil

2/3 cup apricot nectar

3 teaspoons lemon extract

4 eggs

1 yellow (or lemon) cake mix

1 ½ cup powdered sugar

1/3 cup lemon juice

Beat egg whites stiff. Mix: oil, apricot nectar, egg yolks, and lemon extract. Slowly add cake mix. Beat for 5 min. Fold egg whites into mixture. Put into ungreased tube or flat pan. Bake for 45-50 min. at 325˚. While still hot, poke holes in top with a fork. Mix powdered sugar and lemon juice. Then pour over top.
