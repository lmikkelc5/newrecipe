Graham Cracker Crust (Marissa)

1/3 cup margarine or butter ¼ cup sugar 1 ¼ cups finely crushed graham crackers (about 18 crackers) Melt butter. Stir in sugar. Add crushed graham crackers. Toss to mix well. Spread evenly into a 9-inch pie plate. Press onto bottom and sides to form a firm, even crust. Chill about 1 hour or till firm. (Or bake at 375 for 4-5 minutes or until the edge is lightly browned. Cool on a wire rack before filling.)
