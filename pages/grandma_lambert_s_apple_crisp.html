Grandma Lambert’s Apple Crisp

Sift 1/2 cup flour & 1/2 teaspoon cinnamon. Stir in 1/3 cup brown sugar. Cream in 1/3 cup butter. Fill pie tin with apples and cover with crumb mix*. Bake at 350˚ for 1 hour till the apples are tender.

*1/2 cup brown sugar and add 1/4 cup oats

With the apples I mix dry pie filling from the lion house cookbook–halved, or mix in 2-4 tablespoons of sugar and bake for 30 minutes instead.
